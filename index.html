Product Overview

This device combines GSM (SIM900A) and GPS (NEO6M) modules to send your location and alert trusted contacts. Features include an SOS button, automatic call to registered numbers, loud buzzer and a built-in defensive shock module.

How it works

  1. Press the SOS button — the device immediately sends an SMS with GPS coordinates to pre-configured contacts.
  2. The device attempts to call the primary contact number.
  3. A loud buzzer/siren activates to draw attention.
  4. Optional: a brief defensive shock is deployed if the device's tamper sensor is triggered (hardware configured by owner).
